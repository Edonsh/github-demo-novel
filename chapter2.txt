
 Still, there were nights when uncertainty crept into his thoughts. The bright lights 
of his mansion could not completely silence the questions in his mind. He wondered 
whether 
the dream he chased was truly within reach or whether it existed only in the glow of 
memory and imagination.

Yet Catsby refused to abandon hope. The green light continued to shine across the

 remained, Catsby believed the future still held the promise of everything he 
 had ever desired.
